Grade K - Grade 5

At QCA, our foundational years (K-5) serve as a vibrant journey, nurturing the inherent curiosity and eagerness of young learners while building a strong moral foundation based on our faith values.

Grade 6 - Grade 8

Transitioning into middle school (Grades 6-8) marks a period of deeper exploration and structured learning in both academics and religious studies. At this stage, our students delve into an enriched curriculum.

9

Grade 9 - Grade 10

As our students progress into high school (Grades 9-10), our focus pivots towards not only preparing them for the rigors of college-level academics but also introducing them to such courses.

11

Grade 11 - Grade 12

In the culminating years of high school (Grades 11-12), our students embark on a transformative academic experience. Each student is required to take on-site college courses in Math and Science.

Welcome to QCA For Boys

Learning at QCA is a vibrant, ambitious, and adventurous experience meticulously designed for our students . Our faculty, experts in their subjects, dedicate a great deal of time to refining the art and science of teaching. You will always find our classrooms buzzing with conversation and activity.
